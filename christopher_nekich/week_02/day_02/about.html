Though she resides in Jersey City, Jenn Schiffer lives at the intersection of tech and creativity. She is the developer behind Make 8 bit art and creator of visual artwork that has been described as "neon abstract pixel erotica". After achieving success with her own app, Jenn now helps others as the community manager at Glitch and finds the fun in the tech world with her satirical literature. She also teaches art using code and code using art.
