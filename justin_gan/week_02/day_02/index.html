Why does Joel admire Larry Wall?

Larry, much like Joel, also gives zero fucks about visual design and is just as unapologetic about it.

Larry is the Chuck Norris of programming. Creator of the Perl programming language, Larry is a Benevolent Dictator For LifeBenevolent Dictator For Life (BDFL) is a title given to a few open-source software dev leaders who have the final say in community disputes who makes no apologies for his take no prisoners approach to programming.

For example, the official Perl documentation states:

  1. Larry is always by definition right about how Perl should behave. This means he has final veto power on the core functionality.
  2. Larry is allowed to change his mind about any matter at a later date, regardless of whether he previously invoked Rule 1. Got that? Larry is always right, even when he was wrong.
